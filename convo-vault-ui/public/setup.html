Export Messages is a powerful data export tool for GoHighLevel. It helps you:

  • Advanced Filtering: Filter by date range, channel, status, and more
  • Pay Only When You Export: Free to install, no subscription fees

üöÄ Installation

Step 1: Install the App

Click the "Install" button in the marketplace or visit the installation link provided by your administrator.

Step 2: Connect Your Sub-Account

Select the sub-account where you want to install Export Messages. You'll be redirected to authorize the connection.

Step 3: Access Export Messages

After installation, find "Export Messages" in your sub-account's left sidebar menu.

Step 4: Start Exporting

Click on Export Messages to open the dashboard and start exporting your data!

üìò How to Use

Export Conversations

  1. Go to the Conversations tab
  2. Set your filters (date range, channel, status, etc.)
  3. Click Export to get a price estimate
  4. Confirm and pay to start the export
  5. Download your CSV or JSON file when ready

Export Messages

  1. Go to the Messages tab
  2. Set your date range and filters (max 1 month)
  3. Click Export to get a price estimate
  4. Confirm and pay to start the export
  5. Download your complete message data

View Export History

  1. Go to the Exports tab
  2. View all your past exports with status
  3. Download completed exports anytime
  4. Track progress of ongoing exports

‚ùì FAQs

How much does it cost?

Free to install! You only pay when you export. Base price is $0.0025 per item with volume discounts up to 60% off for larger exports.

What are the volume discounts?

The more you export, the more you save: 10K+ items get 20% off, 50K+ items get 40% off, and 100K+ items get 60% off!

What file formats are supported?

We support CSV and JSON formats for exports. Choose the format that works best for your needs.

Is there a date range limit?

Yes, exports are limited to a maximum of 1 month date range to ensure fast and reliable exports.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption, secure token-based authentication, and never store your conversation data permanently.

Can I re-download my exports?

Yes! All completed exports are available in the Exports tab. Download links are valid for 7 days and can be regenerated.
