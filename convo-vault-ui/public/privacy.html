How ConvoVault protects your data

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

At ConvoVault, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our conversation management application.

In Short: We only access your conversation data to provide the Service. We don't store your messages permanently or share them with third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • User ID, email, and name from your CRM platform
  • Sub-account and company information
  • OAuth tokens for API access (encrypted)

2.2 Usage Data

  • API requests and access logs
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error logs for debugging

2.3 Conversation Data (Temporary)

  • Conversation metadata during search operations
  • Message content during export operations (deleted after export)
  • Uploaded CSV/Excel files during import (deleted after processing)

2.4 Support Communications

  • Support tickets and email communications
  • Screenshots or attachments you provide

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your requests (search, export, import)
  • Authenticate your access via OAuth
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Improve and optimize the Service
  • Detect and prevent security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Retention

4.1 What We Store Permanently

  • OAuth tokens (encrypted)
  • Import job history (metadata only, no message content)
  • Installation records
  • Support ticket history

4.2 What We Don't Store

  • Your conversation messages (temporary only)
  • Contact information from imports (processed and discarded)
  • Exported data (generated on-demand)

4.3 Temporary Data

Conversation and message data accessed during search/export operations is cached temporarily and automatically deleted within 24 hours.

5. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: JWT-based secure token authentication
  • Authorization: All API endpoints require valid authentication
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against abuse and DDoS attacks
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized
  • Access Control: Users can only access their own sub-account data

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or conversation data to third parties.

6.2 Service Providers

We may share data with:

  • Hosting Provider: AWS (for application hosting)
  • Database Provider: MongoDB Atlas (for storing OAuth tokens and job history)
  • Email Service: For sending support communications

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Download your data at any time
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the app to stop data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@vaultsuite.store

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage for:

  • Session management (authentication tokens)
  • User preferences (active tab, display settings)

We do NOT use third-party tracking cookies or analytics that collect personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vaultsuite.store
  • Website: https://convo.vaultsuite.store
